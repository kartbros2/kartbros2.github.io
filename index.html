🎮 How to Play Kart bros

The goal is simple: be the first to complete your chores without getting sabotaged by the house or your friends. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Choose Your Player: Select your character and get ready for the chaos.
  • Complete Tasks: Follow the on-screen prompts to do chores like making breakfast, ironing clothes, or hammering nails. Each task requires quick button presses.
  • Avoid Hazards: The house is full of dangers! Watch out for falling objects, slippery floors, electrical shocks, and other unexpected traps.
  • Sabotage Friends: In multiplayer mode, you can interact with the environment to trigger hazards and slow down your opponents.
  • Winning: The first player to successfully complete a set number of chores wins the round. Survive the madness and claim victory!

For the best experience, play in fullscreen mode to catch every hazard coming your way.
